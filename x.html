Services

Welcome to DopeData An open data community platform providing data at your fingertips. DopeData helps you in collecting data according to your requirements or tasks. For example, collecting data for Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Feedback, Surveys, etc.!

Features

DopeData

DopeData is a data collection service, where requesters request for their data requirement which goes live on the app and contributors can contribute to that data collection and earn perks and points!

How it works?

DopeData provides a two-way beneficial service where requesters get their requested data in an effective way and contributors will also get benefited by contributing to the data.

Have a data requirement?

If you have a data requirement, just put up a request on our app and it will go live on our app after a sanity check of the request.!

Any sorts of data?

DopeData provides all sorts of data like audio, images, videos, text, etc. and for any machine learning and deep learning tasks, Company feedback, Surveys, Product Reviews, etc.

Being cautious?

Be Aware of what data you are providing. DopeData is cautious of data it collects, please do not provide any personal data that you feel as confidential.

Benifits

DopeData rewards you with points for contributing and keeps a track of your level which will help you earn badges for your contribution level. Frequent vouchers will also be given for the best contributors. The more data you provide, the higher your level will be and higher points will be given. You can redeem your points for some awesome premium tools and softwares, only if you have done valid entries.
